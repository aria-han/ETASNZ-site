(Some) Music can be distractive!

Listening to the "wrong" music can negatively impact the quality of your study session! Despite seemingly being able to multitask, our brains are actually constantly switching our focus from one task to another when we try to concentrate on more than one thing, which can disrupt our flow. Listening to music with lyrics tempts you to "sing/hum along", and will distract you from studying properly. However, if you listen to lyricless songs such as LoFi or instrumental music, you can enter a state of calm. Why not give it a try now?
